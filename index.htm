Potato pancakes

  • Level: Easy
  • Total Time: 30 mins
  • Servings: 12

Ingredients:

  • 10 medium potato, peeled
  • 3 small onion
  • pinch of salat
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • oil for frying
  • pepper

How do you make potato pancakes?

  1. Grate the potatoes and onion using a box grater or a food processor fitted with a grating blade. Place the grated potatoes and onion in a clean kitchen towel and squeeze out as much liquid as possible.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the grated potatoes and onion with the beaten eggs, flour or matzo meal, salt, and pepper. Mix well until everything is evenly combined.
  3. Heat the oil
  4. When the oil heats up, place the dough in the pan
  5. Fry the pancakes in batches for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and crispy. You may need to add more oil between batches.
  6. Once cooked, transfer the pancakes to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.
  7. Serve with your favorite additions
Enjoy your meal
